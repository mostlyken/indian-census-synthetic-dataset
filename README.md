## üìã Column Descriptions

### Geographic Identifiers
- **District code**: Unique numerical code for each district
- **State name**: Name of the state
- **District name**: Name of the district

### Population Demographics
- **Population**: Total population
- **Male**: Male population
- **Female**: Female population
- **SC**: Scheduled Caste population
- **ST**: Scheduled Tribe population

### Literacy Statistics
- **Literate**: Total literate population
- **Male_Literate**: Literate male population
- **Female_Literate**: Literate female population
- **SC_Literate**: Literate Scheduled Caste population
- **ST_Literate**: Literate Scheduled Tribe population

### Age Groups
Population distributed across age brackets:
- **Age_Group_0_29**: Population aged 0-29 years
- **Age_Group_30_49**: Population aged 30-49 years
- **Age_Group_50**: Population aged 50+ years
- **Age_Group_0_6**: Population aged 0-6 years (children)

### Workers & Employment
- **Workers**: Total working population
- **Male_Workers**: Male workers
- **Female_Workers**: Female workers
- **Main_Workers**: Main workers (employed for majority of the year)
- **Marginal_Workers**: Marginal workers (employed for less than 6 months)
- **Non_Workers**: Non-working population

### Worker Categories
- **Cultivator_Workers**: Agricultural cultivators
- **Agricultural_Workers**: Agricultural laborers
- **Household_Workers**: Household industry workers
- **Other_Workers**: Workers in other sectors

### Households
- **Households**: Total number of households
- **Household_size_1_person**: Single-person households
- **Household_size_2_persons**: 2-person households
- **Household_size_3_persons**: 3-person households
- **Household_size_4_persons**: 4-person households
- **Household_size_5_persons**: 5-person households
- **Household_size_6_8_persons**: 6-8 person households
- **Household_size_9_persons_and_above**: 9+ person households

### Housing & Amenities
- **Main_source_of_lighting_Electricity**: Households with electricity
- **Main_source_of_lighting_Kerosene**: Households using kerosene
- **Main_source_of_lighting_Solar_Energy**: Households using solar energy
- **Main_source_of_lighting_Other_sources**: Other lighting sources
- **Main_source_of_lighting_No_Lighting**: Households without lighting

### Water & Sanitation
- **Location_of_drinking_water_source_Near_the_premises**: Water source nearby
- **Location_of_drinking_water_source_Within_the_premises**: Water source within premises
- **Main_source_of_drinking_water_**: Various water sources (tap, well, river, etc.)
- **Bathing_facility_within_the_premises**: Households with bathing facilities
- **Latrine_facility_within_the_premises**: Households with toilet facilities

### Assets & Ownership
- **Ownership_Owned**: Owned houses
- **Ownership_Rented**: Rented houses
- **Type_of_bathing_facility_**: Bathing facility types
- **Type_of_latrine_facility_**: Toilet facility types
- **Assets_**: Various household assets (radio, TV, computer, telephone, bicycle, car, etc.)

### Economic Indicators (Power Parity)
Income distribution across purchasing power parity brackets (in Rupees):
- **Power_Parity_Less_than_Rs_45000**: Annual income < ‚Çπ45,000
- **Power_Parity_Rs_45000_90000**: Annual income ‚Çπ45,000-90,000
- **Power_Parity_Rs_90000_150000**: Annual income ‚Çπ90,000-150,000
- **Power_Parity_Rs_150000_240000**: Annual income ‚Çπ150,000-240,000
- **Power_Parity_Rs_240000_330000**: Annual income ‚Çπ240,000-330,000
- **Power_Parity_Rs_330000_425000**: Annual income ‚Çπ330,000-425,000
- **Power_Parity_Rs_425000_545000**: Annual income ‚Çπ425,000-545,000
- **Power_Parity_Above_Rs_545000**: Annual income > ‚Çπ545,000
- **Total_Power_Parity**: Total households with power parity data

### Additional Aggregated Metrics
Various combined metrics for age groups, worker types, and income brackets.
